# Image search brief — copy everything below the line for ChatGPT / Pinterest

---

**Site context:** Personal brand site for a software / cloud / data engineer (Dhinesh Babu). Tone: professional, calm, credible — not startup-bro, not overly “AI stock photo.” Prefer **real photos of you** or **subtle abstract tech** over cheesy handshake/laptop-in-face clichés.

**How many images:** **14** main marketing images — **6** site-wide + **8** story chapter posters (+ you can reuse portrait elsewhere).

---

## 1) HOMEPAGE — HERO (large visual, desktop right column)

**Purpose:** First impression next to headline — “I build systems.”

**Good directions:**
- You at a desk with monitor(s), side or ¾ angle, natural light
- Clean workspace + laptop + maybe second screen (code or diagrams blurred)
- Abstract: minimal server/cloud/pipeline illustration, dark navy-friendly palette
- Architecture whiteboard or notebook sketch (readable blur OK)

**Aspect ratio:** **4:3** (e.g. 1200×900). Horizontal/landscape works best.

**Pinterest / search keywords:**  
`minimal developer workspace aesthetic`, `software engineer desk setup natural light`, `cloud infrastructure abstract dark blue`, `clean laptop desk side view professional`, `engineering notebook sketch systems`

**Avoid:** Generic “AI brain,” neon hacker, stock models pointing at empty screens.

---

## 2) HOMEPAGE — BEYOND WORK (training / life section)

**Purpose:** Matches text about **BJJ, CrossFit**, balance outside work — human and authentic.

**Good directions:**
- BJJ gi / mat (no need to show faces if privacy)
- Gym barbell, kettlebells, or training space (moody but clean)
- Outdoor run or simple “active life” shot that feels real, not catalog

**Aspect ratio:** **3:2** (e.g. 1200×800). Landscape.

**Pinterest / search keywords:**  
`brazilian jiu jitsu aesthetic training`, `minimal gym photography moody`, `crossfit gym cinematic`, `athlete training silhouette`, `martial arts mat detail photo`

**Avoid:** Overly posed fitness models; pick something that could look like *your* routine.

---

## 3) ABOUT — PORTRAIT (headshot)

**Purpose:** Trust and recognition on **About** section + **/about** page.

**Good directions:**
- **Professional headshot:** shoulders up, neutral or soft background, natural smile
- Match clothing to how you want to be seen (polo / shirt, no busy patterns)
- Good light, sharp eyes; you’ll crop **square**

**Aspect ratio:** **1:1 square** (export ~832×832 or larger original).

**Pinterest / search keywords:**  
`professional headshot men simple background`, `linkedin portrait natural light`, `corporate portrait minimal grey background`, `tech professional headshot casual`

**Avoid:** Heavy filters, sunglasses, busy backgrounds, extreme wide angle.

**Purpose:** Thumbnail for **deployments / CI/CD / releases / infra**.

- Terminal aesthetic with green success indicators (staged, readable)
- Cloud + arrows / “automation” feel, **calm** not chaotic

**Aspect ratio:** **16:10** (e.g. 1600×1000). Landscape.

**Pinterest / search keywords:**  
`CI CD pipeline abstract illustration`, `devops automation minimal dark`, `software deployment concept abstract`, `infrastructure as code aesthetic`, `release management visual metaphor minimal`

## Rights & quality (important)

- **Best:** Photos **you took** or **you commissioned**; headshot from a photographer.
- **Stock / Pinterest finds:** Check **license** (commercial use). Prefer **Unsplash, Pexels, paid stock** with clear terms — not random Pinterest reposts without source.
- **Resolution:** Start **larger** than needed; the site can crop. Hero ~**1200px+ wide**, cards ~**1600px+ wide**, portrait **1000px+** square original.
